# Semantic Shield — Privacy Policy

**Last updated:** June 2026  
**Extension name:** Semantic Shield  
**Contact:** Use the support email listed on the Chrome Web Store listing.

## Summary

Semantic Shield does **not** collect, sell, or transmit your personal data to the developer or to third parties. All extension data stays **on your device** in your browser’s local storage.

## Data We Do NOT Collect

We do not collect or transmit:

- Personally identifiable information (name, email, address, etc.)
- Health, financial, or payment information
- Passwords or authentication credentials
- Personal communications
- Precise location or GPS data
- Browsing history sent to our servers (we have no servers for this purpose)

## Data Stored Locally on Your Device

The extension uses the browser `storage` permission to save **only on your device**:

- Your feature toggle settings (on/off preferences)
- A local block list of nuisance elements/URLs you or the extension blocked
- A local whitelist for items you chose to restore

This information is **not uploaded** to the developer and is **not shared** with third parties.

## How the Extension Works on Web Pages

When you visit a webpage, content scripts may run on that page to:

- Restore client-side copy, paste, and right-click restrictions
- Highlight hidden close buttons
- Block nuisance ads and auto popups
- Manage the local block list

Processing happens **in your browser**. We do not receive the content of pages you visit.

## Remote Code

Semantic Shield does **not** load JavaScript from remote servers. All code is included in the extension package.

## Third-Party Services

The extension does not integrate third-party analytics, advertising, or tracking SDKs.

Optional donation links (e.g. PayPal) on store listings are external websites governed by their own policies; they are not required to use the extension.

## Children

Semantic Shield is not directed at children under 13, and we do not knowingly collect data from anyone.

## Changes

We may update this policy. The “Last updated” date will be revised when changes are made.

## Contact

For privacy questions, use the **support email** on the Chrome Web Store developer listing for this extension.
